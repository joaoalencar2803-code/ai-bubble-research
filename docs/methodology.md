# Research Methodology

This project adopts an interdisciplinary approach combining finance, accounting, economics and technology.

## Primary Sources

- SEC filings
- Corporate annual reports
- Quarterly financial statements
- Investor presentations
- Earnings calls
- Macroeconomic databases
- Academic literature

## Analytical Framework

The analysis evaluates:

- Revenue quality
- Cash flow generation
- Capital expenditure
- Return on invested capital
- Balance sheet quality
- Corporate dependency networks
- Energy availability
- Credit conditions
- Market valuation

## Validation

Whenever possible, financial information is verified using multiple independent sources before inclusion.

Numerical estimates are clearly distinguished from factual observations.

Historical comparisons are used for analytical purposes and do not imply deterministic forecasts.
