# AI Infrastructure Bubble Thesis

Research repository for the study **"The Financial Architecture of the AI Infrastructure Bubble"**.

## Overview

This repository contains research, analysis, data, and supporting material for a macro-financial thesis examining whether the current AI infrastructure investment cycle represents a sustainable technological transformation or a speculative capital allocation bubble.

The research focuses on:

- Financial statements and accounting analysis
- Capital expenditure (CapEx) trends
- GPU and data center economics
- Cloud infrastructure
- AI hyperscalers
- Semiconductor industry
- Private credit and SPV financing
- Energy constraints
- Macroeconomic conditions
- Market valuation
- Systemic financial risk

## Research Questions

- Is AI infrastructure investment economically sustainable?
- Are current valuations supported by future cash flows?
- How dependent is the ecosystem on continuous capital inflows?
- What are the main systemic risks?
- Which historical bubbles provide useful comparisons?
